MCARC History

The Macon County ARC had its start back in the 1950's in Bevier, MO when a group of young Hams came together at the Rock Tavern and organized the first official Radio Club in Macon County. In the beginning the core of the MCARC were the Radio Operators of the Missouri Highway Patrol Troop B.
